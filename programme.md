# Practical Information 

In Palmer G01-05 (or the foyer) you will find:

* All catering/refreshments, unless otherwise specified.
* Oxford University Press.
* Routledge.
* British Philosophical Association.

Though not a fully **quiet room**, you are welcome to relax in the
conference office (Palmer G06). Various classrooms in Edith Morley
-- especially philosophy room G73-- are likely to be empty.

The Palmer lecture theatres (G10 and 109) are **air-conditioned**, and
you are welcome to relax or work in them when they are not in
use. The coffee shops have A/C.

There is a **water bottle refill** station on the ground floor foyer of Palmer.

For the canonical programme and logistics advice see
sites.google.com/view/2026-joint-session
