# Practical Information 

Though not a fully quiet room, you are welcome to relax in the conference office (Palmer G06). 

Various classrooms in Edith Morley (especially philosophy room G73) are likely to be empty. 

The two Palmer lecture theatres (G10 and 109) are air-conditioned, and you are welcome to relax or work in them when they are not in use. 

Coffee shops including Dolce Vita, in the Library, and in the Henley Business School) have A/C. 

There is a water bottle refill station on the ground floor foyer of Palmer.
