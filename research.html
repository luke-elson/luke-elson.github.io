Research

Unsharp Choices

Most of my research focuses on a cluster of issues around incomplete preferences, unsharp or imprecise credences, and value incommensurability. I am working on a book manuscript, tentatively titled 'Unsharp Choices', which argues that these all involve vagueness, and that seeing them as such solves a number of puzzles.
