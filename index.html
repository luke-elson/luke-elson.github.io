Publications and Research

At the moment I’m focusing on two projects. The first concerns evaluative incomparability or incommensurability, and whether it is vagueness. The second is a defence of Global Normative Nihilism—the theory that we don’t have any reasons or justification for anything we do or believe. I’m especially concerned with the question of whether this is a depressing view.
